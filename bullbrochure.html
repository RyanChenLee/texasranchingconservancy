Discover "The Advantages" of
Rangeland bulls Today!


The Advantages:

  • Active but manageable temperaments

  • Born naturally, out on the range, without any calving assistance

  • Out of a cows that have been regular breeders, since their first birthday

  • Out of cows with good to excellent udders

  • Raised and performance tested under native range conditions

  • Highly acceptable on the basis of weight and visual appearance (conformation) when evaluated as yearling bulls

  • Are easy keepers and do not require an excessive supplementation and pampering to stay in good body condition

  • Are physically sound and have the ability to breed cows successfully as long-yearlings.

  • Are aggressive breeders and exhibit exceptional herd bunching ability

  • Are highly prepotent and have the ability to transmit their functional traits to their offspring

  • Sire calves that

    • have low birth weights
    • are strong enough to get up and suck shortly after birth
    • are thick and meaty
    • grow off fast
    • flesh well on lightly stocked range


  • Sire calves that develop into highly acceptable slaughter animals and functionally superior herd replacements

  • Are highly resistant to disease and parasites

  • Are optimum in size when mature, reflective of a bull that can sire herd replacements best suited to the rancher's particular type of rangeland ranching environment

It's a simple proposition -- turning-out Rangeland bulls with
your cows will improve the functionality of your herd
