Sustainable ranching systems

  • Profitable Ranching in Harmony with Rangelands: A Sustainable Method of Ranching by Nol Ward -- This article sets forth a method of ranching that is financially viable, ecologically responsible, and socially acceptable, and can be sustained over an indefinite period of time without degrading and fragmenting our country's agriculturally and ecologically important rangelands.

Recommended government programs

  • Ranchers Need Financial Help and Education to Practice Sound Range Management: What Government Can Do: A Proposal to U.S. Congress by Nol Ward -- This article sets forth a basic plan of action that enhances the financial health of country's range cattle ranchers, and the ecological health of our country's privately owned rangelands.

Texas ranching history

  • Texas range report (1898) by H.L Bentley -- This report provides an excellent overview of Texas range conditions in the early days of the Texas range cattle industry.
